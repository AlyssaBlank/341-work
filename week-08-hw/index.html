Katara and Moose Overcome All Odds in Quest for Home!

Throughout the cold winter of 2016, Katara and Moose, the great house kitties of the northwest, set out on a wondrous journey to Missoula. The story of their journey to Missoula is a great one!

Katara

The great kitty Katara, was first sighted on Facebook. Her beautiful hair and playful nature were the most adorable features. After a long week, Katara's personality had done her best to captivate her new audience. She was soon to find new hope with her new family! With just days to wait until meeting them, Katara contniued to play with her puppy friends, who taught her a silly habit of chasing her very long tail. Katara was picked up in Coeur d'Alene, where she stayed with her host family that rescued her from the streets.

Moose

Moose began his great journey in Pinehurst, Idaho. He started life as an outside cat that would have to defend himself. His worst enemies in the outdoors world were: wolves, large birds like eagles, bears, and coyotes! Without them, Moose may have lived a simply life outside, hunting mice. But, Moose's destiny was to become a big tabby kitty that lives to eat, indoors.

Together!

The great kitties' next journey brought them together on a cold winter night. Katara was picked up first, and traveled to Moose's home next. Moose, offended by the other kitty's presence, hid in the back seat all the way to Missoula! He must have thought he was the superior kitty.

Pals Forever

The following encounters were definitely a stressful few weeks at their new home, for Moose wanted to be the top kitty. Katara, however, was not letting it happen at all. The kitties Moose and Katara, kitties of the great Missoula, Montana. Now, Katara and Moose are practically brother and sister, with all their lives ahead of them and much kitty chow to eat. There is nothing better than Kitty Pals that can keep you company at any time!
