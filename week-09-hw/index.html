What is it about Kitties?

Kitties all around the world continue to be a great choice for your family pet. They bring instant joy to anyone that enjoys seeing the 'little' tigers running about. Over the last 15 years, kitties have become an internet craze!

The activites that one can enjoy with their kitties are numerous. Kitties love climbing about things, especially boxes and kitty trees. Some folks on the internet have found that kitties are afraid of silly things, like cucumbers, while others have found that kitties will do other silly things like sit inside of circles made of tape.

It is unfortunate to see how many kitties go un-loved in their cages at humane societies and other institutions like that. This is certainly a byproduct of kitty owners not getting their kittens spayed or neutered and winding up with extra bundles of kitties!

Recently, AniMeals hosted a kitty awareness night at a local brewery in Missoula. It is great to see activist organizations like that who take initiative and attempt to solve the kitty population problem that the world faces daily.


Long live the House Kitty!
